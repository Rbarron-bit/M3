Food accommodations

FOOD & BEVERAGES

Our food program features top food trucks from across the country offering a wide variety of food that pleases any festival-goer. We did not forget about vegan and gluten-free options. Bars located throughout the festival will serve beer and liquor. ID required to buy alcohol. Cash, credit, debit, Apple Pay, and Google Pay will be accepted.

Each of M3's stages represents a distinct union of technology and today's world.

From the pyrotechniques and the stage to the sound and the performers, so many unique elements go into bringing this world to life. We invite you to wander, explore, interact, and connect.

    Embassy Suites Hotel & Spa

    The Embassy Suites Hotel & Spa is the ideal place for relaxing spa breaks, leisurely vacations and local business trips. This all-suite hotel is host to some of the most spectacular golf courses in the country. Relax in a two-room suite offering thoughtful amenities such as WiFi, two televisions, and telephones in both the living room and bedroom. Enjoy the view of the pool from your patio. Enjoy the ambiance of the modern water feature during the complimentary breakfast or Evening Reception. The open-air atrium is also home to Terra Bistro, serving lunch and dinner daily. This inviting space boasts several seating areas where guests can view the big screen TV or chat with friends, family or colleagues.
